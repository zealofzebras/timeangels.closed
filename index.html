Time:Angels

Time:Angels has been shutdown indefinitly

Time:Angels was a volunteer/advisory initiative focused on helping early-stage and impact-driven startups grow by connecting them with experienced advisors for short, focused sessions. The project facilitated conversations, introductions, and advisory sessions that gave startups practical guidance, diverse perspectives, and access to networks they might not otherwise reach.

Core activities

  • Matching startups with experienced advisors and mentors for short advisory sessions.
  • Running events, calendars and community meetups to surface opportunities and connections.
  • Supporting sustainable and impact-first startups with strategic advice and introductions.
